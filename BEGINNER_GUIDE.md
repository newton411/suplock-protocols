# SUPLOCK for Beginners - Quick Guide

## What is SUPLOCK? (60 seconds)

SUPLOCK is a money-making machine disguised as a DeFi protocol. You lock your $SUPRA tokens, and in return:

1. **You earn yields** (12-42% APY)
2. **You earn protocol fees** (share in 35%+ of all trading revenue)
3. **You control the protocol** (governance voting)
4. **You help build a price floor** (supply decreases → price goes up)

Think of it like a cooperative bank where members vote on decisions and share profits.

### 1. LOCK (Commitment)

### 2. VOTE (Control)
```
What: Use veSUPRA to vote on protocol decisions
Decisions: Fee splits, vault parameters, treasury spending
Power: 1 veSUPRA = 1 vote
Impact: Your vote directly affects your future earnings
```

### 3. YIELD (Growth)
```
What: Deposit assets into automated vaults
Options: Stable (12.4%), Balanced (18.9%), Advanced (42.1%)
Earn: APY + protocol fees
Advanced: PT/YT tokens for capital flexibility
```

### 4. COLLECT (Rewards)
```
What: Claim your protocol fee share
Source: All transaction fees converted to USDC
Frequency: Monthly distributions
Amount: 35% of fees go to veSUPRA holders
```

## FAQ: Common Questions

**Q: Is locking safe? Can I lose my money?**
A: Your locked $SUPRA is protected by smart contracts and the 10B price floor. You can unlock early (with a penalty), but your principal is secure.

**Q: What if I need my money before 48 months?**
A: You can unlock early, but you lose unclaimed yields and pay a penalty. Better: Don't lock more than you can afford to keep locked.

**Q: Do I get taxed on yields?**
A: Yes, this is taxable income in most countries. Consult a tax professional.

**Q: How do I know my vote matters?**
A: Every veSUPRA = 1 vote. If 100M veSUPRA exist and you have 17.5M, you have 17.5% voting power. Your vote WILL move proposals.

**Q: When do I get fee distributions?**
A: Monthly. Go to Reserve page and claim your USDC rewards.

**Q: What happens if the protocol fails?**
A: The 10B floor gives you price protection. Even if protocol stops, your tokens are still worth at least 10B ÷ supply.
